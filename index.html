In October 2016, Reach4Change connected 6 different people from 5 countries and different backgrounds at Spotify Headquarters in Stockholm, to innovate a better world for children and women. This is the outcome of our work trying to solve one of the biggest problems in our world. The initiative was born as a complementary help to HOPE, a project in Senegal of Jean Luc, a 23 year old social entrepreneur that wants to give infants and mothers, the same opportunity as he had when a blood donation saved his life after a car crash when he was 5.
